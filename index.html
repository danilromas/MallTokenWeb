What is
Virtual Token Mall?

Web3 platform

Virtual Token Mall is a Web3 platform for online shopping center stakeholders, providing users with the ability to manage interactions between physical and digital assets through unique, collectively generated, 3D representations of their shopping centers.

The Digital Twin Token: The shopping center’s unique digital copy

Our platform uses blockchain technology to record essential data, enabling transparent, traceable, and immutable streams of information to be readily accessible to its users. Virtual Token Mall smart contracts are built with this blockchain data to expedite tasks like lease agreements, services arrangements, and customer interactions.

An Index of digitization

The Virtual Token Mall Index provides valuable insight for decision-making regarding data-sharing, service-creating, and operational/environmental expectations in the online shopping center industry.
